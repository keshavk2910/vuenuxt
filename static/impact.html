Our Impact

Better Lives in a Greener Haiti

We envision a greener, more resilient Haitian landscape. A future where farmers not only survive, but can thrive because of access to seeds, training, and markets. A future where Haitians and their public institutions have the knowledge and the means to effectively manage their natural resources.

Haiti Takes Root was launched during the historic climate summit in 2015 that led to the Paris Agreement, uniting countries around the world to fight climate change. Despite its relatively small contribution to climate change, Haiti is one of the most vulnerable to this global phenomenon. Haiti is no stranger to being a leader in fighting for justice, and today Haiti is part of a global campaign for environmental justice.
